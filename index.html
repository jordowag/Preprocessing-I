About Me

Born in 20XX, Dr. Thomas Light created me and my sister Roll to help out in the lab. I was Dr. Light's lab assistant and Roll helped with housekeeping. We were the first line of robots with advanced AI, with abilities to make our own decisions based on orders and direcitons.

Dr. Light created industrial robots to help society. However, Dr. Wily took control of these robots for his own goal: world domination. I stepped up and asked Dr. Light to equip me for battle in order to fight against Dr. Wily. Ever since then, I have been an advocate for justice and peace, hoping that one day robots and humans can live together in harmony.
